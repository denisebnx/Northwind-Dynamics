# Northwind-Dynamics
You play the new identity engineer at Northwind Dynamics, a fictional 4,000 person company running Okta, Entra ID, CyberArk, and Workday. The home screen is a mission board of 13 challenges — one per IAM Academy discipline — that you can tackle in any order. Each is graded pass/fail with XP and completion tracking, exactly like the Academy labs, and progress saves in your browser.

This is where the course stops teaching and starts testing. Every mission drops you into a simulated enterprise tool to do the real job: triage a broken SSO login, process a department transfer, vault a privileged credential, tune Conditional Access, script an off-boarding, audit the directory for risk, and more. Clear all 13 and you've run identity at a company end to end.

Enjoy!
